# SQL Learning Roadmap: Beginner to Advanced

Welcome to your SQL journey! This roadmap is designed to teach you SQL from the ground up, preparing you for real-world development and interviews. We will cover each topic in carefully structured phases.

## How this works:
1. Each topic includes a simple explanation, syntax, a real-world example, common mistakes, and practice questions.
2. You will learn step by step. I will not jump ahead.
3. You must solve the practice questions before I give you the answers!
4. The difficulty will gradually increase as we progress.

## The Roadmap

* **Phase 1 – SQL Fundamentals** (What is SQL, SELECT, WHERE, ORDER BY, LIMIT)
* **Phase 2 – Filtering & Data Operations** (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE, BETWEEN, IN, LIKE)
* **Phase 3 – Aggregation** (COUNT, SUM, AVG, GROUP BY, HAVING)
* **Phase 4 – Joins** (INNER, LEFT, RIGHT, FULL, SELF, CROSS)
* **Phase 5 – Advanced SQL** (Subqueries, CASE, COALESCE, NULLIF)
* **Phase 6 – Window Functions** (OVER, ROW_NUMBER, RANK, LEAD, LAG)
* **Phase 7 – Performance & Optimization** (Indexes, EXPLAIN, Normalization, ACID)
* **Phase 8 – Real World Queries** (Sales reports, Top N queries, Pagination)

Let's begin with Phase 1! Check the first lesson inside the `phase-1` directory.
